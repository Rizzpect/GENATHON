How It Works

From camera input to posture feedback in milliseconds

01

Capture

Upload an image or use your webcam to capture your current sitting posture in real time.

02

Detect

YOLOv8 pose estimation identifies your body and locates 4 critical keypoints on your torso and shoulders.

03

Classify

The model analyzes keypoint positions to classify your posture as Good or Bad with confidence scoring.

04

Feedback

Receive instant visual feedback with annotated results, confidence gauge, and actionable posture insights.
